Al Anon - Hackney Friday meeting

About the meeting

Hackney Friday is a face-to face meeting that happens on Friday evenings in Hackney, London, UK.

Time

We meet from 18:30 to 19:45 - often people stay afterwards to share conversation and fellowship.

Meeting

We meet in the church hall. As you look at the church the hall is on the right set back a little - it has its own entrance (no need to go in the church itself). We put Alanon signs up in the windows. Please come in through the double doors and the hall is on the right.

Our meeting address is:

St John The Baptist Church Hall, 3 King Edwards Road, Hackney London, E9 7SF, England.

Accessibility

Al Anon is for everyone. We welcome everyone regardless of race, gender, sexuality or age. The room is wheelchair accessible and there is plenty of space. The toilets are up a few stairs on the left as you come in. We welcome well behaved dogs too.
